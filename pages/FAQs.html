What is the procedure for borrowing a book ?

To borrow a book, please choose the desired book and click on the "Borrow" option. You will receive a notification indicating the book's availability in the library. If the book is not available, kindly visit the library, where the librarian will assist you.

What is the duration of borrowing a book ?

The borrowing duration depends on the nature of the book:

  1. If the book pertains to your current semester subject, it will be available for you until the conclusion of the semester exams.

  2. If the book is not related to your current semester, the librarian will provide you with the specified borrowing duration.

How can I see my book borrowing history ?

To review your borrowing history, log in to your Readify account on the library website. Navigate to the user dashboard, where you will find a section displaying your borrowing history.

How long does it take to receive the book I've booked ?

The processing time for reserved books may vary. Typically, books are ready for pickup by the end of the classes. You will be notified when the book is available for collection. If the book is not available on the same day as your reservation, you will receive a notification accordingly.

Do I need to notify before returning a book ?

While it's not mandatory, notifying the library in advance about your intention to return a book can help streamline the process and ensure that staff is available to assist you promptly. However, it's not a strict requirement.

Is there a limit on the number of books I can borrow at once?

Yes, there is usually a limit on the number of books you can borrow simultaneously. Library policies may vary, so please check with library staff for specific borrowing limits.

What happens if I return a damaged book?

Returning a damaged book may incur fines or replacement charges. The extent of charges depends on the severity of the damage. Please inquire at the circulation desk for details.

Are there any late fees for overdue books?

Yes, late fees may apply for overdue books. The amount and duration can vary, so it's advisable to check with the library staff for specific late fee policies.

How much do I have to pay if I lose a book?

The replacement cost for a lost book is determined by the library. Please check with library staff for specific details on replacement fees.
